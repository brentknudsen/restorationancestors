Church History Timeline: 1820 - 1853

  • Spring 1820
    The First Vision

  • Sep 22, 1827
    Joseph Smith receives gold plates

  • May 15, 1829
    Joseph Smith and Oliver Cowdery receive Aaronic Priesthood

  • Jun 1829
    The Book of Mormon translation is complete

  • Apr 6, 1830
    Church is organized

  • Aug 3, 1831
    Joseph Smith dedicates the temple site at Independence, Missouri

  • May 8, 1834
    Zion's Camp

  • Mar 27, 1836
    Kirtland Temple dedicated

  • Jul 20, 1837
    The first mission in the church in the British Isles

  • Oct 27, 1838
    Gov. Boggs issued an order to exterminate or expel the saints from Missouri.
    Joseph Smith and others were made prisoners of the Militia.

  • Apr 25, 1839
    Commerce, Ill. (Nauvoo) was selected as the gathering place for the Church

  • Jun 27, 1844
    Joseph & Hyrum Smith were killed by a mob in Carthage, Illinois

  • Dec 10, 1845 - Feb. 7, 1846
    Some 5,000 members received their endowments
    in the yet to be finished Nauvoo Temple

  • Feb 4, 1846
    The Mormon Migration from Nauvoo began

  • Jul 13, 1846 - July 16, 1847
    The Mormon Batallion

  • Feb 14, 1853
    Brigham Young broke ground for the Salt Lake Temple
